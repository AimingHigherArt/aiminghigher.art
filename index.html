Meet Brandon

Built from passion. Driven by purpose.

"I don't just want to make something beautiful. I want to make something that matters to someone."

Aiming Higher was born from a simple belief — that the things we keep close should mean something. Brandon founded this studio with a background in design, a heart for people, and a laser that could turn raw materials into lasting memories.

Every project starts with your vision. Every piece is crafted with care. And every finished creation is offered back as an act of gratitude for the gifts we've been given.
